Good to know

What does "+ domain" mean?
Your plan price covers the design and build. Domain registration (e.g., yourname.com) is a small annual fee paid to a registrar — usually $10–20/year. We help you set it up.
What's the 20-hour cap?
Each plan includes up to 20 hours of work. Basic caps at $400, Regular at $700, and Premium at $1,000 total. Anything beyond is billed at our hourly rate.
How does upkeep work?
After launch, we can maintain and update your site for $7/month on retainer, or $20 per individual update. No long-term contracts required.
What are photoshoots for?
We photograph your business, team, or products to use as custom imagery on your site — way better than stock photos. Additional sessions are $15 each after your included ones.
